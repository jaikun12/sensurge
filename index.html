Know when disaster strikes. We got your back.

A web platform that allows you to optimize supply routes for your business, steering away your goods from the risks of sudden natural disasters.

Paired with a mobile app, sensurge helps drivers know their routes towards their destination. In a single click, other users on the otherhand could provide a report if a natural disaster has occured in their location. Users who provided accurate reports will be rewarded.

Monitor your supply routes with the help of the sensurge web platform. Be informed of route changes that are made by the system to steer away your valuable goods from natural disasters.
Our platform does not only consider flood as the problem, but also other risks such as driver visibility, and road wetness. Sensurge automatically reroutes your supply lines into the closest and safest route towards its original destination.

Use the power of analytics to your advantage. All data gathered related to the performance of your supply routes are uploaded into the cloud. These data are analyzed to improve the performance of your supply lines.
Sensurge analytics introduces heatmaps, delivery performance comparison, and route ratings to provide support to decision makers.

Sensurge is a prototype developed on Hack The Climate: Business Unusual which happened last September 18, 2016 in the Philippines with over 30 participating teams.

The goal of the hackathon is to create an application to help businesses cope up with climate change.

Sensurge finished at the 8th place.
